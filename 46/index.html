Celebrating Our Time Together

Dear Sharon,

Today marks our incredible journey of 3 years and 10 months together. Though we may be physically apart right now, distance can never diminish the love that binds us.

You're far away, yet always near,

In my heart, you're crystal clear.

Though I miss you every day,

Love you, miss you, I want to say.

As the days pass by, and time draws near,

Counting down the weeks, my dear.

Memorial weekend, we'll finally embrace,

Our love's reunion, a cherished space.

Thank you for being the love of my life. I eagerly await the moment we can be together again.

With all my love,

David
